About Synthetic Mass

Synthetic Mass is an open-source Health Information Service Provider (HISP) populated with 7 million realistic “synthetic residents” of Massachusetts.

Synthetic Mass provides:

  • Realistic population health and demographic data at the city and town level
  • The ability to drill down to individual synthetic patient data
  • Fictional but realistic data representing synthetic providers and patients
  • APIs and Data Services such as Direct, FHIR and CCDA
